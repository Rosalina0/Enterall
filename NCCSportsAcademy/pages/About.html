General Information


NCC Sports Academy is a current and ongoing project created by the academy NCC Sports. It is an academy which at its inception the sporting activities included football,hockey,rugby,ballet and gymnastics. The Academy operates a unique programme which allows its participants to experience understand the values of the Sportsman's lifestyle, as well as to grow and succeed in life and play professionally at the highest level. The Academy's goal is to keep the community involved in its activities and give them to opportunity to plan their own sports events.
